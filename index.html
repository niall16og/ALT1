Meeting The Brief

Analysis Of Brief

Home and school environments refers to the overall conditions and factors present in a household or school, including physical surroundings, comfort, safety and cleanliness.


Keywords


Please identify an everyday issue in either a home or school environment that you feel could be improved or eliminated. The objective is to solve the issue with the use of an embedded system (micro:bit) and the writing of a functional program. You can use the block code from micro:bit or python.


Everyday Issue

Improved or Eliminated

Embedded System

Functional Program

The issue must be something that affects everyday life in the home or school

The issue/problem must have a viable solution that either sufficiently reduces or entirely removes the effect of the problem from the environment.

The solution must involve an embedded system, such as a microbit, that will be programmed to perform the specific task required to solve the problem.

The solution must involve a suitable, working program to take inputs and give appropriate outputs that contribute to the solution.

Key Aims and Objectives

  • Use and control digital inputs and outputs within an embedded system

  • Measure and store data returned from an analogue input

  • Develop a program that utilises digital and analogue inputs

  • Design automated applications using embedded systems

  • Create a suitable solution to an everyday issue that exists within a home or school environment

  • Improve the experience for the individual(s) in the chosen environment.
